👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 KeenKeeper — Keep Your Friendships Alive
KeenKeeper is a smart relationship management tool designed to help you track and nurture your personal connections. Never let a friendship fade — KeenKeeper reminds you when it's time to reach out.

1. 👫 Smart Connection Management

Dashboard Overview: See all your friends at a glance with summary cards showing total connections, on-track relationships, and those needing urgent attention.
Dynamic Status Logic: Friends are automatically categorized as On-Track, Almost Due, or Overdue based on your last contact date and personal communication goals.


2. 👤 Friend Detail Page
A clean two-column layout giving you deep insight into each connection:

Real-time Stats: View days since last contact, your goal interval, and the next due date.
Quick Check-In: Log interactions instantly — Call, Text, or Video with one click.
Action Buttons: Snooze, Archive, or Delete a connection directly from the detail page.


3. 📜 Interaction Timeline
A dedicated history page that keeps a full log of every interaction.

Updates automatically when a check-in is performed.
Helps you track communication patterns over time.


4. 📊 Friendship Analytics
A visual Pie Chart built with Recharts on the Stats page, showing the breakdown of your communication methods — Call, Text, and Video — so you can understand how you connect best.
